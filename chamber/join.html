Membership Levels

Non-Profit Membership Level

Non-Profit Membership Level

Cost: Free for qualifying businesses

Benefits:

  • Directory listing with nonprofit designation
  • Promotion of fundraising events on Chamber calendar/website
  • Access to networking events and community partnerships

Bronze Membership Level

Bronze Membership Level

Cost: $30.00 monthly

Benefits:

  • Basic business listing in Chamber's online directory
  • Invitation to Chamber networking mixers & monthly luncheons
  • Eligibility for inclusion in Chamber-wide email updates
  • Discounted rates for attending Chamber events/workshops

Silver Membership Level

Silver Membership Level

Cost: $80.00 monthly

Benefits:

  • Enhanced listing in the Chamber's online directory (with logo & link)
  • Discounted booth fee at Chamber expos/markets
  • Two spotlight posts per year on Chamber social media
  • Access to Chamber referral program
  • Invitations to member-only networking events & trainings

Gold Membership Level

Cost: $80.00 monthly

Benefits:

  • Featured listing at the top of the Chamber's online directory
  • Priority placement in Chamber publications & promotional materials
  • Complimentary sponsorship of one major Chamber event per year
  • Dedicated spotlight post on Chamber's social media channels (quarterly)
  • Complimentary use of Chamber meeting room space
