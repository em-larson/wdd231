Final Project Site Plan

Content

Site Name: Ogden D&D Compendium, a possible domain name could be ogden-dnd.com

Site Purpose: This will be a one stop guide for people in the Ogden area that are intereseted in dnd. It will contain ways to get in contact with nearby groups that are looking for members as well as a starting guide for people who are interested in picking dnd up as a hobby. It will contain a store and local get togethers as well.

Scenarios: Possible questions that could be asked at this website contain: "Is there a group nearby that I would be able to join?" "What if I've never played, how can I learn?" "What supplies do I need to get started?"

Color Schema: A main color would be a dark grey to match (or be close to) the official dnd material. A secondary color for headings and a title would be a bright reddish orange color.
https://www.figma.com/color-palette-generator/?colors=8C0808-F5F1F0-1D1F1D-7D7978

Typography: The fonts will be Amarante Static for the titles and headers, and Markazi Text for the paragraph font.
